Solar Dispatch Hub Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 15, 2026

Solar Dispatch Hub ("the Extension") is an internal browser extension built and operated by Cosmo Solar Co LLC, doing business as Solar Dispute ("we", "us", "Solar Dispute"). It is distributed privately to authorized Solar Dispute team members and is not offered for general public use. This policy explains what the Extension accesses, how that information is used, and how it is handled.

Single purpose

The Extension's single purpose is to notify Solar Dispute staff of new leads, inbound client messages, and calls in the company's GoHighLevel CRM, and to give staff quick actions for responding to them.

Information the Extension accesses

To do this, the Extension reads data from systems that already belong to Solar Dispute:

  • Lead and client records from the company's GoHighLevel (LeadConnector) account, including names, phone numbers, email addresses, message content, conversation assignment, and call activity. This is used to build the in-extension notification feed and the missed-call list.
  • For internal setter calls, the Extension may route call audio to the company's Google Drive for transcription, used for internal quality and record-keeping.
  • The team member's own selected identity within the Extension, used to label which client records are assigned to that member.

How information is stored

Notifications, related identifiers, configuration, and the team member's selected identity are stored locally in the browser using Chrome's storage. This local data remains on the team member's device until it is cleared or the Extension is removed.

How information is used and shared

Information accessed by the Extension stays within Solar Dispute's own first-party systems, GoHighLevel and Google Workspace (Google Drive and Google Apps Script). Specifically, we:

  • do not sell or transfer this information to third parties outside of providing the tool's stated function;
  • do not use or transfer it for any purpose unrelated to the Extension's single purpose;
  • do not use it for advertising, profiling, or determining creditworthiness or lending eligibility.

The personal information the Extension processes belongs to Solar Dispute's leads and clients and is handled solely to operate Solar Dispute's customer-communications workflow.

Permissions

The Extension requests access to GoHighLevel (services.leadconnectorhq.com), Solar Dispute's own application domain (solardispute.com), and Google Apps Script (script.google.com and script.googleusercontent.com) in order to read CRM data and route files and transcripts to Solar Dispute's Google Workspace. Standard browser permissions (storage, notifications, alarms, tabs, offscreen) are used to poll for updates, display alerts, and process audio for transcription.

Data retention

Local data persists in the browser until it is cleared or the Extension is uninstalled. Lead, client, and call data continue to be governed by Solar Dispute's retention practices within GoHighLevel and Google Workspace.

Changes

We may update this policy as the Extension changes. The effective date above reflects the latest version.
